## Cause of loss — observations and conclusions
System is a natural gas-fired hot water boiler with radiant heating equipment. Manufactured by Bryant, approximately 3 years old. The boiler's heat exchanger failed as a result of freezing — the boiler was exposed to freezing temperatures and water within the system froze and expanded. The radiators were also exposed to freezing temperatures and water within the piping froze and expanded. No other components were affected. HVACi's opinion: cause of loss to the boiler is Freezing Conditions.

## Recommendation
Full replacement of the damaged HVAC system required to restore pre-loss condition — replace the boiler and affected radiators.
